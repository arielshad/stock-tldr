<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>

</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Warsh was confirmed 54–45 (narrowest margin on record), succeeding Jerome Powell who stays on as a governor through January 2028. Trump said "I want Kevin to be totally independent" at the ceremony, then publicly called for rates to come down "very quickly" at a later rally.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
Warsh inherits a 30Y at 19-year highs, CPI at +3.8% Y/Y, and a June 16-17 FOMC — where the first dot plot under his tenure will either validate or reject the Trump-favoured cut path. April FOMC minutes showed 8-4 hold, most dissents since October 1992.
</p>

<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
Macro traders, rates desks, Fed-independence watchers.

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">SEC blinks on tokenized stocks — exchanges flag shareholder-rights gaps and the agency holds the Innovation Exemption past its planned May release.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
The SEC paused the rollout of its Innovation Exemption framework for tokenized stocks after meetings with stock-exchange officials and market participants raised investor-protection concerns. SEC staff had been on track to release the framework as soon as this week.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
The most contentious piece was allowing third-party tokens — blockchain representations of shares issued without the underlying company's involvement — which triggered exchange-side concerns over how dividends, voting, and shareholder rights would be administered. The framework is delayed, not formally withdrawn.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
The tokenized-stocks framework was the centerpiece of the Atkins-era "crypto-onto-Wall-Street" agenda — a near-term catalyst for COIN, Robinhood, and on-chain RWA products. A pause pushes out spot tokenized equities and clouds Wall Street partnerships with Nasdaq, Cboe, and CME.
