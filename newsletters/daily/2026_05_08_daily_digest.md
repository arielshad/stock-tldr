<h2 style="margin: 0 0 8px; font-size: 24px; font-weight: 800; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.2;">Akamai Q1 2026 — revenue $1.07B in line, but $1.8B/7-yr AI cloud commitment from frontier-model provider sends AKAM ~+26%</h2>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">Boring earnings, blockbuster customer disclosure: a frontier-model lab just signed Akamai's largest deal ever.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
Akamai posted Q1 2026 results with revenue of $1.074B (+6%) and non-GAAP EPS of $1.61, both in line with Street, but separately disclosed that a leading U.S.-based frontier model provider committed $1.8B over seven years for Cloud Infrastructure Services — the largest customer contract in Akamai's history.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Security revenue grew 11% to $590M, Cloud Infrastructure Services accelerated to +40% Y/Y at $95M, and the legacy Delivery business shrank 7% to $389M. Akamai guided Q2 revenue of $1.075–$1.10B and FY26 of $4.445–$4.55B.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
Akamai had been an awkward AI story — a CDN bleeding to hyperscalers — but the frontier-lab contract reframes the cloud-infrastructure unit as a credible neocloud. AKAM closed up ~26% to ~$147, a 52-week high, with read-throughs to other AI-adjacent infra names like CRWV, NBIS, and APLD.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
AI-infrastructure investors and cloud-CDN watchers tracking neocloud deal flow.
</p>
