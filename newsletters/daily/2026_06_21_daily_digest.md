<h2 style="margin: 0 0 8px; font-size: 24px; font-weight: 800; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.2;">Hormuz Tanker Traffic Surges to 20 Ships on June 18 — Highest Since June 2 — as Saudi VLCCs Carry 6M Barrels and Iranian Supertankers Turn AIS Back On</h2>

<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
Kpler vessel-tracking data shows 20 tanker transits of the Strait of Hormuz on Thursday June 18 — the highest single-day total since June 2 — including 3 Saudi-flagged VLCCs operated by Bahri carrying ~6M barrels.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
