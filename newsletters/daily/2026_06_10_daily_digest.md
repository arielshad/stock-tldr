<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">May headline CPI hits a 3-year high of 4.2% as the Iran-driven energy shock filters through gasoline prices — core stays contained at 2.9%.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
The Bureau of Labor Statistics released the Consumer Price Index for May 2026 on June 10 at 8:30 a.m. ET. The all-items index rose 0.5% M/M and 4.2% Y/Y — matching consensus and the highest annual reading since April 2023.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Energy did the heavy lifting: the energy index rose 3.9% M/M and 23.5% Y/Y, with gasoline alone +40.5% Y/Y as the Strait of Hormuz closure feeds through retail pump prices. Core CPI rose a softer-than-expected +0.2% M/M and +2.9% Y/Y.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
The 4.2% print is the hottest in three years and arrives ahead of the June 16–17 FOMC under Kevin Warsh. Treasuries sold off — the 10Y yield pushed to ~4.53% — and fed-funds futures lean toward zero cuts in 2026 with rising odds of a hike.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
Macro, rates and FX traders ahead of the June 16–17 FOMC.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">GSK pays a 40% premium for two next-generation lung-cancer drugs with FDA decisions in September and November 2026.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
GSK announced a definitive agreement on June 9 to acquire Cambridge, MA-based oncology biotech Nuvalent for $124 per share in cash — total equity value $10.6B, net of cash $9.4B. GSK will launch a tender offer within 10 business days and expects to close in Q3 2026.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Nuvalent's two late-stage assets are zidesamtinib (selective ROS1 inhibitor, PDUFA September 18) and neladalkib (selective ALK inhibitor, PDUFA November 27) — both for non-small cell lung cancer. GSK said the deal will not change its 2026 core EPS growth guidance of 7–9%.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
NUVL gapped ~+38% to ~$122 in U.S. premarket while GSK fell ~3% in London on dilution risk. The deal pulls Nuvalent off the table before its first commercial launches, continuing a wave of mid-size pharma M&A targeting oncology platforms with near-term FDA catalysts.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
Biotech M&A traders, healthcare allocators, oncology specialists — and peers like Revolution Medicines watching the bid premium read-through.
