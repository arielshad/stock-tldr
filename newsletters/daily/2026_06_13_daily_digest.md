<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>

The Division said the transaction 'is not likely to result in harm to competition' and 'would increase competition across the media and entertainment ecosystem.'
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Federal clearance removes the Washington antitrust ceiling on a combined entity that would control HBO Max, CNN, Comedy Central, Nickelodeon, and the Paramount and Warner Bros studio labels. The tender-offer expiration was extended to July 1, 2026; PSKY rose +2.77% to $10.62 and WBD rose +0.45% to $26.98 on the day.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
California AG Rob Bonta confirmed the state probe is still active: "The merger of Warner Bros and Paramount is not a done deal and remains under investigation by my office." The multi-state antitrust track — California and New York telegraphed a joint suit on June 5 — is now the binding constraint, not Washington.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">Sentiment ticks up off the record floor as gasoline relief and Iran de-escalation push 1-year inflation expectations back to 4.6%.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
The University of Michigan's preliminary June 2026 consumer sentiment rose to 48.9 from May's record-low 44.8 — the first monthly increase in four months and well above the 46.0 consensus. The improvement was broad across age, income, education and political groups.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
Current Economic Conditions rose to 48.4 from 45.8; the Expectations sub-index jumped to 49.3 from 44.1. Survey director Joanne Hsu credited "some relief due to the early-month easing in gasoline prices," while 5-10y long-run inflation expectations fell to 3.4% from 3.9%.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
Lower long-run inflation expectations matter more to the Fed reaction function than a single sentiment beat. Still, 48.9 is the second-lowest reading in series history — 13% below January 2026 and 19% below June 2025 — and Warsh's first FOMC meeting is four days away on June 16–17.
